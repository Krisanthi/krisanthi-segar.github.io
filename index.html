I'm a 2nd year BSc AI & Data Science student at the Informatics Institute of Technology, affiliated with Robert Gordon University. I design and ship end-to-end ML systems: multimodal RAG architectures with OpenSearch hybrid search, real-time emotion-aware chatbots using DeepFace and Whisper, and distributed PySpark pipelines on AWS.

My focus is making models production-ready. I've worked hands-on with the AWS console provisioning Glue jobs, Lambda functions, Step Functions orchestration, and DynamoDB tables. I've built RAG pipelines with two-stage re-ranking, integrated Llama 3.3 via Groq for inference, and deployed Streamlit apps to production.
